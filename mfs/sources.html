“One​ ​of​ ​the​ ​greatest​ ​accomplishments ​of​ ​my​ ​life​ ​has been​ ​using​ ​my God​-given​ ​ talents​ ​to​ ​see​ ​my​ ​dreams​ ​come​ ​true.​ ​ ​It​ ​is my​ ​passion​ ​to​ ​inspire​ ​and​ ​motivate​ ​my​ ​family,​ ​friends, and​ ​other​ ​stylists​ ​that​ ​are​ ​eager​ ​to​ ​learn​ ​and​ ​devoted to​ ​ the​ ​natural​ ​hair​ ​art​ ​form.”

As​ ​an​ ​educator​, ​Stasha​ ​has​ ​participated​ ​in​ ​several natural​ ​hair​ ​events,​ ​including​ ​the​ ​Natural​ ​Hair Industry​ ​Convention in Baltimore​ ​Maryland. To date, she has also done several features with Shake-N-Go, Creme of Nature ​and Cosmopolitan Magazine. With​ ​her entrepreneurial​ ​spirit,​ ​she​ ​has​ ​produced​ several​ ​in-salon​ ​workshops​ ​featuring​ ​the​ ​most​ ​trending​ ​braid techniques.

Stasha​ ​M.​ ​Harris​ ​is​ ​just​ ​beginning​ ​her​ ​entrepreneurial adventure. Her​ ​vision​ is​ ​to​ ​open​ ​a​ ​full​-​service​ ​natural hair​ ​salon/spa​ ​ ​with​ ​massage​ ​and​ ​nail​ ​services.​ ​She wants​ ​to​ ​offer​ ​the​ ​community​ ​a​ ​“one​-​stop​ ​shop”​ ​that’s​ ​clean,​ ​proficient​ ​and​ ​ professional.​ ​Her​ ​salon/spa will​ ​be​ ​a​ ​nurturing,​ ​relaxing​ ​space, ​where​ ​the​ ​clients’ time​ ​will​ ​be​ ​honored​ ​and​ ​minimized.
