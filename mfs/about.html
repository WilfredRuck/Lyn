​Stasha​ ​M.​ ​Harris

Celebrity​ ​Stylist,​ ​Salon​ ​Entrepreneur,​ ​Educator

Stasha​ ​M.​ ​Harris​ ​has​ ​been​ ​styling​ ​hair​ ​for​ ​as​ ​long​ ​as she​ ​could​ ​ remember. At an early age, she ​always​ ​knew​ ​she​ ​wanted​ ​to be​ ​a​ ​ hair stylist​ ​and​ ​entrepreneur.​ ​​Her​ ​biggest supporter​ ​has been ​her​ ​mother​ ​who​ ​got​ ​her​ ​an​ ​internship as​ ​an​ ​assistant​ ​at ​a​ ​local​ ​hair​ ​salon​ ​ in​ ​Trinidad​ ​when she was just 13 years old.​ ​At​ ​the​ ​age​ ​of​ ​16​, ​she​ ​arrived​ ​ in​ ​New​ ​York ready​ ​to​ ​attain​ ​financial​ ​freedom,​ ​independence​ ​and industry​ ​ knowledge.​ ​She​ ​received​ ​her​ ​training​ ​at​ Empire Beauty​ ​School.​ ​As​ ​a​ ​New​ ​York​ ​State​ ​licensed cosmetologist​, ​Stasha​ ​has​ ​been​ ​trained​ ​on​ ​ multi-textured​ ​hair,​ ​nails,​ ​and​ ​skin;​ ​but​ ​fell​ ​in​ ​love​ ​with​ ​the full​ ​ range​ ​of​ ​creativity​ ​of​ ​naturally​ ​textured​ ​hair​ ​and protective​ ​styling.

In​ ​2009,​ ​at​ ​the​ ​age​ ​of​ ​21, Mrs. Harris​ ​opened​ ​Tempted To​ ​Touch​ ​Salon​ ​in​ ​ Brooklyn,​ ​where​ ​she​ ​is​ ​currently the​ ​Lead​ ​Stylist​ ​and​ ​Educator​ ​for​ ​her​ ​ 8​ ​natural​ ​hair technicians.​ ​Her salon has since been renamed “MagicFingersStudio”, of which a second location has opened on October 15th, 2017. Stasha​ ​is​ ​known​ ​for​ ​her​ ​signature sculpting​ ​artistry,​ ​where​ ​her​ ​ skillfulness​​ ​in​ ​parting takes​ ​on​ ​an​ ​art​ ​form.​ ​She​ ​specializes​ ​in​ ​the​ ​“Empress” Feed​-​in​ ​Braids​ ​that​ ​are​ ​trending​ ​all​ ​over​ ​the​ ​world.

Mrs.​ ​Harris​ ​is​ ​an​ ​international​ ​social​ ​media​ ​influencer with​ ​approximately​ 167​ ​thousand​ ​ ​Instagram followers.​ ​As​ ​a​ ​stylist​ ​influencer,​ ​​she​ ​has​ ​created​ ​a large​ ​clientele​ ​base​ ​from​ ​all​ ​over​ ​the​ ​world.​ ​Stasha’s signature​ ​natural​ ​hair​ ​styling,​ ​including​ ​locs​ ​and braiding,​ ​has​ elevated​ ​the​ ​engagement​ ​and conversation​ ​about​ ​protective​ ​styling​ ​and​ ​textured hair.

As​ ​a​ ​celebrity​ ​stylist,​ ​she​ ​has​ ​done​ ​numerous​ ​models, dancers​, ​and​ ​actors,​ ​including​ ​Shameik​ ​Moore.​ She was​ ​also​ ​a part​ ​of​ ​a​ ​social​ ​experiment​ ​where​ ​her​ ​work was​ ​featured​ ​on​ ​VH1.

“One​ ​of​ ​the​ ​greatest​ ​accomplishments ​of​ ​my​ ​life​ ​has been​ ​using​ ​my God​-given​ ​ talents​ ​to​ ​see​ ​my​ ​dreams​ ​come​ ​true.​ ​ ​It​ ​is my​ ​passion​ ​to​ ​inspire​ ​and​ ​motivate​ ​my​ ​family,​ ​friends, and​ ​other​ ​stylists​ ​that​ ​are​ ​eager​ ​to​ ​learn​ ​and​ ​devoted to​ ​ the​ ​natural​ ​hair​ ​art​ ​form.”

As​ ​an​ ​educator​, ​Stasha​ ​has​ ​participated​ ​in​ ​several natural​ ​hair​ ​events,​ ​including​ ​the​ ​Natural​ ​Hair Industry​ ​Convention in Baltimore​ ​Maryland. To date, she has also done several features with Shake-N-Go, Creme of Nature ​and Cosmopolitan Magazine. With​ ​her entrepreneurial​ ​spirit,​ ​she​ ​has​ ​produced​ several​ ​in-salon​ ​workshops​ ​featuring​ ​the​ ​most​ ​trending​ ​braid techniques.

Stasha​ ​M.​ ​Harris​ ​is​ ​just​ ​beginning​ ​her​ ​entrepreneurial adventure. Her​ ​vision​ is​ ​to​ ​open​ ​a​ ​full​-​service​ ​natural hair​ ​salon/spa​ ​ ​with​ ​massage​ ​and​ ​nail​ ​services.​ ​She wants​ ​to​ ​offer​ ​the​ ​community​ ​a​ ​“one​-​stop​ ​shop”​ ​that’s​ ​clean,​ ​proficient​ ​and​ ​ professional.​ ​Her​ ​salon/spa will​ ​be​ ​a​ ​nurturing,​ ​relaxing​ ​space, ​where​ ​the​ ​clients’ time​ ​will​ ​be​ ​honored​ ​and​ ​minimized.
